Correlation of p-caught and p-rec in Story Pairs

This report summarizes the analysis of story pairs: stories that share a common structure or theme (Schissel, Triplett, Hester). The goal was to examine whether probability of being caught (p-caught) and probability of recall (p-rec) correlate between the two versions of each pair.

Method

Scatter plots were prepared for each story pair, comparing p-caught and p-rec across the two versions. This allows inspection of whether segment-level recall statistics are aligned between paired stories.

Findings (May 2025)

It is worth noting that there is no correlation for either p-caught or p-rec in the Hester story. The Schissel and Triplett pairs show their own patterns in the scatter plots.
