Costa Story: Multimedia and Dataset Packaging

This report documents work on the Costa story in its multimedia form: illustration, audio, and packaging for sharing. The goal was to provide an illustrated, audio version of the story for recall experiments and to distribute a self-contained dataset (Jul–Aug 2025).

Purpose

The Costa multimedia dataset supports experiments where subjects experience the story via audio and images (rather than text only). It is used in the “Again” experiments (e.g. immediate vs one-week delayed recall with multimedia input). The dataset is also intended for review and reuse by collaborators.

First illustration attempt (July 2025)

Illustrating a longer story like Costa is more complex than short narratives: characters and scenes reappear, and keeping visual consistency is difficult. In addition, text-to-speech and speech-to-text pipelines had limitations. Despite that, a first set of illustrations was produced manually and used to build an initial multimedia presentation in the hope it would help design better versions later.

Dataset archive

A zip archive containing the sources (including a large mp3 file, ~11 MB) is provided for download:

Costa dataset (costa.zip)

Download is recommended for full offline use and for reproducing the multimedia setup.

Limitations

Illustrations are not perfect; character and scene consistency across the story remains challenging. TTS/STT tooling also introduced errors. The current assets are a first step toward better multimedia stimuli for recall experiments.
