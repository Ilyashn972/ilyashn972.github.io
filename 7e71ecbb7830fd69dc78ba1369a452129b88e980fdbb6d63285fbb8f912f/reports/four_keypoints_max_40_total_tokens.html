Four keypoints — total budget 40 tokens (calibrated on Costa)

Methodology

Each story is summarized as exactly 4 keypoints with a fixed total token budget chosen by calibrating on costa (longest narrative) so summaries stay comparable in length across stories.

  • Calibrated total token budget: 40
  • Calibration story: costa

Keypoint generation

  • Script: experiments/four_keypoints_calibrated.py
  • Model: gpt-5.5
  • System prompt (total budget N=40):
I will give you a narrative below. Summarize it into exactly 4 key points that together cover the ENTIRE story.

Requirements:
- Each key point must be a simple clause with a subject and a predicate.
- The combined length of all 4 key points together must be at most 40 tokens total (not per key point).
- The 4 key points must collectively summarize the WHOLE narrative: every major episode, plot thread, and turning point. Do NOT focus on only one part of the story.
- Bad example (partial): for a story about a hospital stay and the family's reaction at home, listing only hospital events OR only family reactions.
- Bad example (partial): for a story about a stoning and later police involvement, listing only the stoning OR only the police aftermath.
- Good example: distribute the 4 key points across the full timeline so a reader understands the complete story arc.

Give me 10 such sets of 4 key points. The output must be in the following JSON format:
```json
{
  "samples": [
    {"keypoints": ["keypoint1", "keypoint2", "keypoint3", "keypoint4"]},
    ...
  ]
}
```
  • Output: 10 samples × 4 keypoints per story

Log-probability (logp)

  • Model: meta-llama/Llama-3.3-70B-Instruct-Turbo
  • Text scored: all 4 keypoints joined with spaces
  • Prompt template: <|begin_of_text|>{joined_keypoints}
  • Metric: unconditional sum(logp)

Semantic information (mbits)

  • Model: meta-llama/Llama-3.3-70B-Instruct-Turbo via tools/get_mbits.py
  • Preceding context: full narrative text
  • Text scored: 4 keypoints (each keypoint is one segment)
  • Conditioned prompt template: <|begin_of_text|> The following two texts splitted by '---' tell the same story with different wording: {narrative} --- {keypoints}
  • Metric: sum(mbits) = Σ (logp with narrative context − logp without context) over keypoint segments. Higher values mean the narrative makes the keypoints more predictable.
