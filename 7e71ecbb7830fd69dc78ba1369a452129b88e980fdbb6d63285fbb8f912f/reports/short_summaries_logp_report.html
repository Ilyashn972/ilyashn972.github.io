Short summaries and logp

Narrative hub for experiments that score unconditional sum(logp) of short LLM summaries against narrative length (characters). Compare three summary formats: four keypoints with a calibrated total token budget, and single-sentence summaries capped at 40 tokens or 110 characters.

Reports

  • Four keypoints (max 40 total tokens, Costa-calibrated)
  • Short summaries (max 40 tokens)
  • Short summaries (max 110 characters)

Reproducibility

From the repository root, with dependencies installed (.venv):

cd /Users/ilyashnayderman/projects/llm-narrative-analysis

# Rebuild HTML from existing caches (no API calls)
.venv/bin/python tools/build_four_keypoints_report.py --plots-only
.venv/bin/python tools/build_short_summary_logp_report.py --max-tokens 40 --plots-only
.venv/bin/python tools/build_short_summary_logp_report.py --max-chars 110 --plots-only

To refresh logp scores from Together, add --compute-logp (four keypoints) or --generate-summaries --compute-logp (short summaries) instead of --plots-only.
