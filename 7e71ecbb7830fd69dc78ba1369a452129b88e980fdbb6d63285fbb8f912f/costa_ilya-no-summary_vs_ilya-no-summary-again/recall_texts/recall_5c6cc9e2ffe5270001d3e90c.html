My daughter died 28 years ago. On the 30th of April. My daughter Rita went to the hospital to see Marie and there was a chair at the foot of the bed. She asked the nurses why there was a chair there. They said that Marie would have to sit on it. Marie had blood clots in her leg. She was eighteen. Someone who called me 'Mrs Costa' called me and told me to go to the hospital.
