Small Recall 942 942 Mary's daughter had to go to hospital to have an operation. She ended up being in hospital for 10 days. She was only 18 at the time. She then got the call to come and pick her up. But they didn't have a phone. So the hospital called the local store to pass the message on. When she went to pick up Maria (her daughter) from the hospital, It turned out Maria had died while she was waiting. It was later found out it was a bloody clot that caused the death. Mrs. Costa broke down and was going crazy. Her husband was waiting at home for the mother and daughter to come home. But they didn't. Their other daughter went to the hospital to see where they were. And found out that Maria had died. And she collapsed as well. When they both eventually came back to the house. And informed the family what had happened. Her husband had a heart attack. And so did her son. Her two sisters were also there. And were a mess when they were told the news.

Big Recall 1477 1477 Mrs Costa recalls the day her daughter Marie died, she had gone in for an operation and was given Ether as an anaesthetic. Mrs Costa had waited to get the call from the hospital to come and pick her daughter a store as she did not have a phone then. When she got the call, she went to the hospital and was told to wait by a nurse. After waiting a while a nurse came to ask her if her daughter had had fainting spells before, and then Mrs Costa asked if the reason they were running about frantically was because of Marie to which the nurse confirmed. She rushed into the room and saw Marie sweating heavily, and then the doctor came in, a lady, she was the assistant to the doctor that usually showed up. They did their best but Marie died, and Mrs Costa became hysterical, pulling her hair and screaming, they tried to give her something but she angrily refused, she knew all the nurses by name because she had had three operations that year. Back home her husband had asked about her and her other daughter Rita had explained that she got the call, Rita went to the hospital and got the news and she fainted, when they got home, she told her husband Mike what had happened and he had a heart attack, their son Mike came around, and when he heard the news, he also had a heart attack. Her sisters Millie and Jennie had been waiting for Marie's return, so she sent her son Jamie to go tell them what happened. It was discovered that a blood clot was the cause of Marie's death.
