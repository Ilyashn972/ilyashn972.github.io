Recall vs Summaries Analysis: costa

Analysis Overview:

This analysis examines how different summaries (varying in length) perform at predicting three different recalls of varying complexity:

  • Small Recall (1175 characters, params: A=0.089, B=22.768, x_t=1339.000, c=142.212)
  • Middle Recall (1880 characters, params: A=0.046, B=26.735, x_t=2163.786, c=127.103)
  • Big Recall (416 characters, params: A=-0.008, B=27.386, x_t=3943.000, c=-2.600)

The plot shows log probability sums (y-axis) for each summary length (x-axis). Vertical dotted lines indicate the character length of each recall. The piecewise continuous fit highlights how summary length affects recall prediction quality, with linear growth that saturates beyond a transition point.
