Recall vs Summaries Analysis: costa

Analysis Overview:

This analysis examines how different summaries (varying in length) perform at predicting three different recalls of varying complexity:

  • Small Recall (1175 characters, params: A=0.023, B=29.671, x_t=7517.045, c=199.328)
  • Middle Recall (1880 characters, params: A=0.015, B=34.658, x_t=7143.620, c=144.690)
  • Big Recall (416 characters, params: A=-0.003, B=21.223, x_t=9870.848, c=-8.125)

The plot shows log probability sums (y-axis) for each summary length (x-axis). Vertical dotted lines indicate the character length of each recall. The piecewise continuous fit highlights linear growth that saturates beyond a transition point, revealing how summary length affects recall prediction quality.
